# Attempt 002 — Early Stages of the 林爽文事件

## Focus

Stage 1: the 黃仕簡、任承恩 response before 福康安 became the solution.

## Working hypothesis

The initial failure was both military and informational. The court expected coordinated suppression by Fujian commanders, but fragmented authority and multiple reporting channels produced dispersed defense and delayed strategic correction.

## Track

- People: 黃仕簡, 任承恩, 常青, 柴大紀, 孫士毅, 徐嗣曾, 李侍堯
- Places: 彰化, 大里杺, 府城, 鹿仔港, 諸羅, 鳳山, Fujian rear areas, and the imperial decision center
- Chronology: event, sending, receipt/rescript, and announcement dates
- Relations: reporting routes, command authority, delay, response, and responsibility

## Method

1. Filter and inventory Stage 1 documents.
2. Separate event chronology from document and imperial-knowledge chronology.
3. Identify clusters around major losses, dispatches, and command changes.
4. Compare intended strategy with reported battlefield action.
5. Review model-produced summaries and pairings against quoted evidence.

## Expected outputs

- reviewed Stage 1 document inventory;
- communication and command timeline;
- command-relation table;
- concise interpretation of the initial response failure.

## Open decisions

- Define the exact Stage 1 endpoint.
- Choose the primary chronological view for each document type.
- Decide whether to prioritize broad metadata review or dense document clusters.
