# Terminology

- **Source evidence**: wording or metadata directly present in a registered source.
- **Interpretation**: a researcher’s claim derived from evidence; it must not be presented as source wording.
- **External enrichment**: information added from an authority such as CBDB or CHGIS; keep it separate from source evidence.
- **Formal state**: the accepted working overlay in `review-tools/(1) formal/formal_all.data`.
- **Sample state**: isolated demonstration data in `review-tools/(2) sample/sample_all.data`.
- **Review bundle**: a model-run package awaiting classification or human review.
- **Candidate pairing**: a proposed relationship between documents; it is not accepted evidence until reviewed.
- **`send_date`**: date an upward document was sent.
- **`receive_date`**: date an imperial rescript record was received or answered.
- **`announce_date`**: date an imperial order was announced.
- **Event date**: a date stated inside the document for the reported event; it may differ from document dates.
- **Uncertainty**: an explicit indication that a reading, match, date, identity, or interpretation is not settled.

Use English for explanation by default. Preserve Traditional Chinese source wording and do not silently modernize, translate, or convert it.
