# Working War Stages

| Stage | Working label | Approximate boundary | Central problem |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | 黃仕簡、任承恩分路渡臺 | 乾隆五十一年十一月至五十二年三月 | Early underestimation and fragmented command. |
| 2 | 常青、藍元枚督剿 | 乾隆五十二年三月至十一月 | Reorganized command, stalled reinforcement, and divided fronts. |
| 3 | 福康安統兵反攻 | transition begins around 乾隆五十二年九月 | Concentrated command and cross-provincial intervention. |

These stages are research scaffolds, not fixed periodization. Transition documents may belong to more than one stage. Distinguish appointment, planning, arrival, and battlefield action when discussing command changes.
