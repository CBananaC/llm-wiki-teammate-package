# Research Question

## Current object

Study the 林爽文事件 as a problem of wartime communication and command.

## Main question

How did early Qing reports, imperial responses, and fragmented command arrangements turn an apparently suppressible disturbance into a war requiring exceptional intervention by 福康安?

## Supporting questions

- When and through which documents did the court recognize the scale of the crisis?
- How were 黃仕簡 and 任承恩 expected to cooperate?
- Where do reports show delay, filtering, uncertainty, or competing command channels?
- When did imperial confidence in the initial response break down?
- How did upward reports and downward orders frame responsibility and military urgency?

## Method boundary

Official documents are evidence of communication and bureaucratic framing, not transparent descriptions of battlefield reality. Preserve source wording, date semantics, and uncertainty.
